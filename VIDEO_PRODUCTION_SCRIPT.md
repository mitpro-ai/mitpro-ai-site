# MIT Pro Video Production Script

## Production Rules

- Style: dark cinematic command center, blue AI glow, emerald protection accents.
- Feeling: Bloomberg Terminal + cybersecurity SOC + mission control.
- Avoid: profit claims, win-rate claims, guaranteed results, hype trading language.
- Use: education, awareness, discipline, protection, risk disclosure, human-centered decision support.
- Legal-safe wording: decision-support only, not financial advice, no guaranteed outcomes, users remain responsible for their own decisions.
- Sound direction: Cybersecurity Command Center + Interstellar Emotion.
- Sound: low sub-bass drone, radar pings, barely audible AI data-processing texture, one large MIT Pro reveal impact with a short cinematic tail, then a warm cinematic rise. Audio should start muted/off until the viewer enables it.
- End every video with: `Education. Awareness. Discipline. Protection.`

## Video 1 - 30 Seconds Attention Grabber

Purpose: Instagram, WhatsApp, Facebook, YouTube Shorts.

### Timeline

0-5s:
Text: `Markets Move Fast. Decisions Matter More.`
Visual: fast market motion, noisy chart overlays, red/amber pressure, small MIT Pro watermark.
Sound: Cybersecurity SOC tone - low drone and distant radar pulse.

5-10s:
Text: `Markets move fast. Emotions move faster.`
Visual: compressed panels, warning flashes, information overload.
Legal tone: risk disclosure, awareness, and discipline before action.
Sound: darker scanning pulses, no EDM, no crypto/trading music.

10-15s:
Text: `Observe. Validate. Protect.`
Visual: chaos clears into MIT Pro command interface.
Sound: AI/data-processing riser.

15-23s:
Text: `Market Intelligence. Protection Active.`
Visual: dramatic MIT Pro/GSSA logo reveal, scan pulse, green shield effect.
Sound: large cinematic `BRAAAAAAM` impact, deep sub-bass hit, 0.5 second tail, then intelligence command-center pulse.

18-24s:
Text: `Observation Engine. Validation Engine. Protection Engine.`
Visual: real MIT Pro-style Mission Desk cards: Environmental Assessment, Liquidity, Validation Strength, Observation Engine, Validation Engine, Protection Engine.
Legal line: `Decision-support only. Not financial advice. No guaranteed outcomes.`

24-27s:
Text: `Built For Those Who Want To Learn. Not Follow.`
Visual: calm mission-focused glow, less market chaos.
Sound: Interstellar-style warm cinematic rise with a very subtle choir-like pad.

23-30s:
Text: `Education. Awareness. Discipline. Protection.`
Visual: final MIT Pro logo lockup, website URL, calm command-center glow.
Sound: final bass hit, then 0.75 second silence while the logo remains visible.

Closing:

## Video 3 - 3 Minute Full Demo

Purpose: product walkthrough for serious users and partners.

### Scene 1 - Welcome

Duration: 10 seconds.

Visual:
MIT Pro logo, secure access, command-center background.

Narration:
`Welcome to MIT Pro.`

### Scene 2 - Mission

Duration: 20 seconds.

Visual:
Website mission section, launch page, protection language.

Narration:
`MIT Pro was created to promote education, awareness, discipline, and protection in dynamic market environments.`

### Scene 3 - Live Desk

Duration: 45 seconds.

Visual:
Live Desk, pair, market mode, confidence, liquidity, timing.

Explain:
`WATCH` means observation.
`PENDING` means the system is waiting for confirmation.
`VALIDATION` means the setup is entering the decision-support window.
`BLOCKED` means protection logic detected caution or conflict.

Narration:
`MIT Pro does not treat every market movement as an entry. It helps classify the state of the market before action is considered.`

### Scene 4 - Protection Layer

Duration: 30 seconds.

Visual:
Arjun Protect, unstable market, sideways market, news guard, low confidence.

Narration:
`MIT Pro may identify conditions that warrant additional caution, including unstable movement, low confidence, sideways conditions, or protection events.`

### Scene 5 - Audit Trail

Duration: 20 seconds.

Visual:
History, logs, reason codes, result review.

Narration:
`Every observation and validation can be reviewed with context, helping users learn from process instead of emotion.`

### Scene 6 - Website

`MIT Pro combines platform access, protected onboarding, and a clear mission around awareness and discipline.`
